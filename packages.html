Group Lessons 

If you play with a group of friends and you all have similar skills you want to hone, or if your club wants to set up a clinic, I'm the guy for you. My motto is Coach, Teach, Train.          

Private Lessons 

Private lessons are great if you are just learning and want to take time to get the basics down. They are also a good way to talk strategy for leveling up. Private lessons go great with group lessons because I can help you build your skills while learning how to implement them in a group lesson.
